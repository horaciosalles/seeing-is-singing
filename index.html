SightSing

Interval ear training with a real-time pitch clock.

  1. Scroll the root segment (12 o'clock) to choose your note and octave
  2. Tap any other segment to set the target interval — it plays the note
  3. Press Sing! and sing freely — watch the clock hand; the clock glows when you land on the target
  4. The small dial at 3 o'clock toggles ascending / descending
